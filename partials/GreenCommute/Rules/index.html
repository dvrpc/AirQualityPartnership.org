Show us your #GreenCommute

Use #GreenCommute on social media to be entered into the giveaway.
Four photo submissions will be entered into a random drawing for one of four $50 Dunkin Donuts gift cards.

Take a photo showing us your green commute (ie. walking, bicycling, taking transit, carpooling) and you could win one of four $50 Dunkin Donuts gift cards. Upload your photo to Twitter, Instagram, or Facebook using the hashtag #GreenCommute to enter.

Anyone who takes a photo in the DVRPC region is eligible to participate. The counties in the region include Philadelphia, Bucks, Montgomery, Delaware, and Chester counties in Pennsylvania; and Burlington, Camden, Gloucester, and Mercer counties in New Jersey. Employees of DVRPC, Board Members (appointed and alternate), and their families, are not eligible to win one of the gift cards.

Entries will be accepted from May 1, 2019 through 11:59 PM EST on June 20, 2019.

Prizes

  • Four (4) gift cards for Dunkin Donuts, valued at $50 each

Four contest participants will be awarded one gift card each. The winners will be randomly selected from all entrants, and will be notified by June 21, 2019. The winners’ names will be posted on the Air Quality Partnership webpage at www.airqualitypartnership.org/GreenCommute

Use and Waivers

By entering, you agree to release the rights of your photograph(s) to DVRPC for marketing, advertising, and communications purposes. You will receive credit as the photographer in all instances.

If photos depict recognizable individuals, the photographer must have permission from the person depicted in the photograph. They must also be able to submit a waiver granting permission upon request. If photos depict recognizable individuals under 18 years of age, the photographer must be the child’s legal guardian or have permission from a parent/legal guardian of each child depicted in the photo to submit the entry into the contest. Further, the photographer must also be able to submit a waiver upon request with the parent/legal guardian’s signature.

Submissions must be the sole creation of the photographer. Each entry may not have won previous awards, may not have been published previously, and must not be awaiting publication or consideration in another contest. Additionally, the photo must be suitable for all ages, not obscene or indecent. DVRPC retains the right to dismiss any photos.
